Welcome to North Cotswold Community Radio

 

 

NCCR - Local community radio for the North Cotswolds and Beyond

Welcome to NCCR, the only station in the North Cotswolds, where we broadcast a selection of music, local news stories and programmes that are relevant to the local community. Listen in Now or playback 7 days worth of content

 

This Weeks News

We have a great line-up of programmes for you, for the Pre Xmas week. A very special Friday this week if you are a Motown fan as Rob from 'Anything Goes' will present a great two hour special of Tamla Motown, Unsigned Community is doing a massive run down of his 2012 best Unsigned Bands & finally a great new show starting on Wednesday, Red with Scarlett Tyler. You couldn't ask for more entertainment.

 

New Show on NCCR

A new show called 'Red' hosted by Scarlett Tyler. Great music and even the UK Top 40 show every fortnight. Features include; "What's Going On?" where we discuss the latest films, music and books, the News at 7, and you can even win prizes in our monthly quizzes and send in your song requests throughout the show. Tune in Wednesday's between 6 and 8 at; select nccr and listen live. What's the worst that could happen?
